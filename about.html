About

I am a passionate and motivated individual transitioning from a background in Biology to a career in software development. After earning my Bachelor's degree in Biology in 2019 from Blackburn College, I realized my growing interest in technology and coding, which led me to pursue Harvard's CS50x course. This course instilled in me a strong foundation in the languages and tools of C, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Flask, and has solidified my desire for creative problem solving through code. I am eager to continue growing my skills and contribute to impactful projects. My particular intersection of technology and science may be of benefit, but I am open to exploring all aspects of software development.
